 == Installation ==
1. Unpack the *.zip file and extract the /gbatchtalk/ folder.
2. Drop the ‘gbatchtalk’ folder into your ‘wp-content/plugins’ folder
3. In word press administration panels, click on plug-in from the menu.
4. You should see your new ‘Gtalk’ plug-in listed under Inactive plug-in tab.
5. To turn the word presses plug-in on, click activate.
6. Go to widget link under Appearance tab, Drag & drop Gtalk widget to your desired location in the active sidebar.
